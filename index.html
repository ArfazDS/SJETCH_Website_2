Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI reduce AT&C losses in power grids?
AI reduces AT&C losses by analyzing smart meter data in real-time, predicting localized energy theft, and identifying technical inefficiencies via Digital Twin simulations without requiring new hardware.
What is the EIIP corporate energy solution?
EIIP is an AI-driven SaaS platform that audits organizational energy usage in real-time, identifying wasteful patterns and providing actionable strategies to significantly reduce corporate utility billing.
Do I need to install new smart meters for EIIIS to work?
No. Our architecture is hardware agnostic. It integrates flawlessly with legacy traditional meters, digital meters, AMR, and modern smart-metering infrastructures.
